Design two

UI (User Interface) Design UI design is all about the look and feel of a product. It focuses on the visual aspects and interactive elements that users engage with directly.

Key Elements of UI Design:

1.Visual Design
2.Buttons and Controls
3.Icons and Graphics:
4.Responsive Design
5.Branding

UX design focuses on the overall experience a user has when interacting with a product. It's about making sure the product is easy to use, efficient, and enjoyable.

In summary, while UI design focuses on the surface-level aspects of the product, UX design is about the overall experience and satisfaction of the user. Both are crucial for creating successful digital products.
